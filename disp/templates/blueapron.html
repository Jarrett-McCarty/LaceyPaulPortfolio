Blue Apron

Blue Apron is a meal delivering service composed of healthy recipes. According to my research, I found that Blue Apron’s target audience live fast-paced lifestyles, but want to cook healthy meals quickly and efficiently. Customers are at-home chefs who enjoy cooking healthy meals for either themselves or their families.

Logo

I redesigned Blue Apron’s logo to create a more modern feel for Blue Apron’s target audience of 25-40 years old. This simplistic design is clear and legible on print ads, digital content, and product packaging.

Print Advertisment

For my print advertisement, I wanted to cater to the target audience by highlighting Blue Apron’s 30-minute meals in a creative and interesting way. Blue Apron’s customers want the convenience of a fast meal without sacrificing quality and fresh ingredients. Associating a healthy meal with a clock tells consumers a delicious meal can take 30-minutes or less.

“Prep and cook delicious meals from start to finish in 30-minutes or less. Enjoy our Vegetarian Mexican-Inspired Quinoa Bowl with avocado, squash, and black beans.”
