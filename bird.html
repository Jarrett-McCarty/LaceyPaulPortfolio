Bird

For this mock-up marketing and advertising campaign, my team wanted consumers to view Bird electric scooters as their go-to ride regardless of where they’re going or what they’re doing. Our research found that our target audience wants alternative ways of transportation that’s quick and efficient while avoiding the dreaded fast-walking sweat. This lead to our big idea and overall marketing campaign:

“Don’t sweat the ride.”

Account Executive - Caroline Dee

Copywriter - Erika Mertz

Digital Strategist - Sam Hutchins

Art Director - Lacey Paul

Logo

I created this logo with a sans serif font for a modern feel that catered to Bird’s target market of 18-24 year olds. I also took inspiration from wings of a bird on both the “b” and “d” to create a memorable logo.

Our tagline, “Your go-to ride” would be on all marketing content. “Don’t sweat the ride” would be featured on all campaign content.

Print Advertisment

We designed two 3-page fold out print ads that reflected Bird’s target market, i.e. the busy millennial who’s always on the go. These ads reflect our big idea, “Don’t sweat the ride,” by illustrating everything that Rachel and Greg are sweating then showing them looking their best. They’re not sweating their ride because they decided to make Bird their go-to ride.

I managed both photoshoots photographed, edited, and designed both print advertisements.

Out-of-Home Advertisment

We created an OOH advertisement that featured sweat towels that said, “Don’t sweat the ride,” with a call to action to download the Bird app and get your first ride free. These sweat towels would be handed out in gyms, recreation centers, and sporting events around the country during the warmer months where Bird scooters are located.

We decided to hold a PR Event at the end of a University of Missouri football game where we’d give away our sweat towels in Mizzou’s black and gold. These PR Events could also be held at other college’s sporting events in the university’s school colors.

I designed and created all OOH content.

Instagram Story

We promoted Mizzou’s PR Event through Instagram’s story feature and Twitter.

The Instagram story informed consumers where and when the event was being held in a creative and colorful way.

I designed, created, and posted all story content on Instagram.

Twitter Post

We also promoted the PR Event on Twitter using our campaign hashtag, “Don’t sweat the ride.”

I created all Twitter content.

Instagram Post

We also posted content on Instagram using the campaign hashtag, “Don’t sweat the ride” to grow our social following on social media platforms.

I photographed and edited all Instagram content.
